Music

  • menuFinal Fantasy for Front Ensemble
    This is an arrangement for marimba and vibraphone created for the Hebron HS front ensemble in 2011 that features a few different tunes from the Final Fantasy Series. This arrangment was quite challenging and was made to push some of the more advanced kids in the program.
  • menuPokémon Battle for Front Ensemble
    This is an arrangement for marimba and vibraphone created for the Fossil Ridge HS front ensemble in 2012 that features the battle theme from Pokémon. This arrangment was a two mallet speed exercise with emphasis on chromatic passages.
  • menuWake for HS Marching Band
    This is an arrangement/composition for high school marching band. This show was titled 'Wake' and took the audience on a journey through dealing with the grief of a death. The journey moves from a place of darkness into light and features quotes from many differnt sources spanning from 16th century counterpoint to modern orchestral selections. The music for the battery percussion was written by Ben Moore.
  • menuWintersprints for HS Marching Band
    This is an arrangement/composition for the last movement in a high school marching band show. This show was titled 'aleaffalls' and focused on the transition from Autumn into Winter. This movement is the beginning of winter and features selecctions from Vivaldi's string quartet as well as a concert band piece by Richard Saucedo called 'Windsprints' The music for the battery percussion was written by Ben Moore.
